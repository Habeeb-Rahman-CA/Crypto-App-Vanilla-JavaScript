About Asset

Bitcoin is the world's first decentralized digital currency, created in 2009 by an anonymous individual or group using the name Satoshi Nakamoto. It operates without the need for a central authority, relying on a peer-to-peer network to verify and record transactions on a public ledger called the blockchain. Bitcoin offers a secure, transparent, and borderless way to transfer value, making it a revolutionary asset in the world of finance. As a digital currency, it can be used for various transactions or held as an investment, with its value driven by market demand.
